Nexus 9 Kernel

This is my custom kernel for the Nexus 9 Android tablet (codenamed Flounder) developed by Google and HTC. It's based on the Elementalx kernel developed by flar2. I began working on this kernel to learn Linux kernel development. My goal with this kernel is to improve performance, battery life and stability.
