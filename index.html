Sublime Linux

About

This is my custom kernel for the Nexus 9 Android tablet (codenamed Flounder) developed by Google and HTC. It's based on the Elementalx kernel developed by flar2. My goal with this kernel is to improve performance, battery life and stability.

Features

  • Sublime Active CPU frequency governor
  • Sublime Quiet CPU quiet governor
  • Latest BFQ I/O scheduler
  • Optimized ZRAM
  • 2.0 Amp charging
  • USB fast-charge (optional)
  • Sweep 2 Wake
  • Sweep 2 Sleep
  • CPU overclocking up to 2.5 Ghz (optional)
  • GPU overclocking up to 9.8 Ghz (optional)
  • NTFS and exFAT support
